Stocks

When you buy stock of a company you become an owner of the company. Now, unless you own 20% or more of the company you do not make any power over the company. However, what you do get is either the stock pays dividends and/or increases in value. Hopefully, all of your stock will increase in value but not all stocks pay dividends. Dividends are the extra income the company makes. They give stockholders a portion of the income. However, instead of paying out dividends the company may reinvest into the company which is also a good thing. Thats because the company will grow and make more money, and your stock will increase in worth. But, if a company files for bankruptcy you will lose all your stocks and get nothing in return.
